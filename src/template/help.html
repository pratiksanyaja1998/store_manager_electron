Some Common Question And Answers...

   Que 1: Shortcuts for FullScreen, Refresh and Quit?

   Ans:press ctrl+f for FullScreen, press ctrl+r for Refresh and press ctrl+q for Quit.

   Que 2: How to backup my data?

   Ans:Go to the setting from the menu and goto below and click on the export button and select folder where you want to store it.

   Que 3: How to restore my backup?

   Ans:Go to the setting from the menu and goto below and click on the import button and select backup file.

   Que 4: How to set invoice serial number or invoice serial number prefix?

   Ans:Go to the setting from the menu and click on edit in invoice settings and change as you want.

   Que 5: How to change the logo?

   Ans:Go to the setting from the menu and hover to the old logo then edit button appears click on it and select PNG file that you want to upload.

   Que 6: where i can view my profit/loss?

   Ans:In the overview tab(press alt+1) profit/loss chart is available and also in the total tab you can view monthly profit/loss.

   Que 7: How to generate a product report?

   Ans:press alt+1 or go to the Overview from the menu and scroll down there product report is available click on the generate button to generate a report file.

   Que 8: How to generate a customer report?

   Ans:press alt+1 or go to the Overview from the menu and scroll down there customer report is available click on the generate button to generate a report file.

   Que 9: How to generate an income/expenses report?

   Ans:press alt+1 or go to the Overview from the menu and scroll down there income/expenses report is available click on the generate button to generate a report file.

   Que 10: where i can view previous months profit/loss?

   Ans:In the overview tab(press alt+1) on the top left corner MM and YYYY is available select MM and YYYY that you want to view.

   Que 11: How to add/remove fields from a customer form/table?

   Ans:Go to Customer(press alt+2) from the menu and click on setting icon available in the top right corner then click on the checkbox that you want to add or remove.

   Que 12: How to add/remove fields from a product form/table?

   Ans:Go to Products(press alt+3) from the menu and click on setting icon available in the top right corner then click on the checkbox that you want to add or remove.

   Que 13: How to add/remove products category?

   Ans:Go to Products(press alt+3) from the menu and click on setting icon available in the top right corner and add or remove products category as you want.

   Que 14: How to add/remove extra information from the invoice?

   Ans:Go to the Invoice(press alt+4) and go to the new invoice(press ctrl+i) and click on setting icon available in the top right corner then click on the checkbox that you want to add or remove.

   Que 15: How to add/remove fields from an invoice table?

   Ans:Go to Invoice(press alt+4) and click on setting icon available in the top right corner then click on the checkbox that you want to add or remove.

   Que 16: How to add/remove expense category?

   Ans:Go to Expenses(press alt+5) from the menu and click on setting icon available in the top right corner and add or remove expense category as you want.
